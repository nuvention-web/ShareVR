Team Overview

Welcome to the home of NUvention Team H!

We want to make foreign language learning accessible and natural. Many people do not get an opportunity to practice a foreign language because they do not know native speakers, cannot receive individualized attention, or are otherwise to embarrassed to engage with real world experience.

We think being able to practice in the language environment is the key.

Enter VR Lingo

What if you could learn French in Paris without even leaving your own home?

What if you could receive a personalized learning experience and get feedback anywhere, anytime?

VR Lingo will Make this a reality.
